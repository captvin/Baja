DANTRINDO has more than 20 years history in outdoor furniture mnufacturing business which reflects our high product quality and technical expertise. We’ve been through all kinds of challenges and obstacles, therefore we knew what technology to invest and the sole purpose of why we do it.


Dantrindo Knows Everything About How To Efficiently Mass Produce Your Furniture With Precision
Facilities & Capabilities
With state of the art technology and exceptional mastery of outdoor furniture making, we make a real difference.

3D Scan & 3D Design


The use of 3D scan technology makes it very easy for us to capture the product mockup desired by the customer, then make improvements and enhancements to the 3D design.

Pipe Laser Cuting Machine


With the pipe laser cutting machine, mass production work become very efficient, and further increases the precision of each product

Wood CNC mill


The wood CNC machine, which can engrave as well as cut wood, can replace tasks that were previously only can be done by woodworkers.

Computerised Pipe Bending Machine


Contemporary design requires superb bending skill, thanks to this automatic pipe bending machine, now we can perform bulk bending processes with higher precision than ever before.
